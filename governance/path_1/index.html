Path 1: Community Signaling¶

Parameters:

  • Name: signaling
  • Maximum Deciding: 20 referenda concurrently
  • Decision Deposit: 100 QUAN
  • Prepare Period: 6 hours
  • Decision Period: 5 days
  • Confirm Period: 3 hours
  • Minimum Enactment Period: 1 block (effectively immediate)

Approval and Support Thresholds:

  • Minimum Approval: Linear decreasing curve from 60% to 50%
    • Requires 60% approval at the beginning of the voting period
    • Requires a simple majority (50%) by the end
  • Minimum Support: Linear decreasing curve from 10% to 1%
    • Very low support threshold to encourage participation
    • Only 1% of the total possible votes required by the end of the period

Permissions:

  • Open to all network participants
  • No restrictions on proposal submission or voting
  • Intended for community signals, small treasury disbursements, and non-binding decisions
  • Perfect for gauging community sentiment before proposing more significant changes
