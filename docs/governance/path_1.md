# Path 1: Community Signaling

## Parameters

- **Name**: signaling
- **Maximum Deciding**: 10 referenda concurrently
- **Decision Deposit**: 100 QUAN
- **Prepare Period**: 6 hours
- **Decision Period**: 3 days
- **Confirm Period**: 6 hours
- **Minimum Enactment Period**: 6 hours

## Approval and Support Thresholds

### Minimum Approval
- Linear decreasing curve from 60% to 50%
- Requires 60% approval at the beginning of the voting period
- Requires at least 50% approval at the end

### Minimum Support
- Linear decreasing curve from 10% to 1%
- Requires at least 10% of the total possible votes at the beginning
- Minimum 1% of the total possible votes required by the end of the voting period

## Permissions

- Any account can submit and vote on proposals in this track
- Used for non-binding community sentiment gathering
- Ideal for feature requests, community feedback, and general signaling
