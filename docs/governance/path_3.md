# Path 3: Treasury Medium Spender

## Parameters

- **Name**: medium_spender
- **Maximum Deciding**: 2 referenda concurrently
- **Decision Deposit**: 500 QUAN
- **Prepare Period**: 24 hours
- **Decision Period**: 7 days
- **Confirm Period**: 24 hours
- **Minimum Enactment Period**: 24 hours

## Approval and Support Thresholds

### Minimum Approval
- Linear decreasing curve from 75% to 65%
- Requires 75% approval at the beginning of the voting period
- Requires at least 65% approval at the end

### Minimum Support
- Linear decreasing curve from 25% to 15%
- Requires at least 25% of the total possible votes at the beginning
- Minimum 15% of the total possible votes required by the end of the voting period

## Spending Limits

- Maximum amount per proposal: 5,000 QUAN
- Maximum total per period: 20,000 QUAN
- Minimum proposal amount: 1,000 QUAN

## Permissions

- Any account can submit and vote on proposals in this track
- Used for medium-sized treasury disbursements
- Ideal for development initiatives and community partnerships
