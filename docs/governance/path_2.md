# Path 2: Treasury Small Spender

## Parameters

- **Name**: small_spender
- **Maximum Deciding**: 3 referenda concurrently
- **Decision Deposit**: 200 QUAN
- **Prepare Period**: 12 hours
- **Decision Period**: 5 days
- **Confirm Period**: 12 hours
- **Minimum Enactment Period**: 12 hours

## Approval and Support Thresholds

### Minimum Approval
- Linear decreasing curve from 70% to 60%
- Requires 70% approval at the beginning of the voting period
- Requires at least 60% approval at the end

### Minimum Support
- Linear decreasing curve from 20% to 10%
- Requires at least 20% of the total possible votes at the beginning
- Minimum 10% of the total possible votes required by the end of the voting period

## Spending Limits

- Maximum amount per proposal: 1,000 QUAN
- Maximum total per period: 5,000 QUAN
- Minimum proposal amount: 100 QUAN

## Permissions

- Any account can submit and vote on proposals in this track
- Used for small treasury disbursements
- Ideal for community grants and small project funding
