# Path 0: Protocol Improvements & Parameter Adjustments

## Parameters

- **Name**: signed
- **Maximum Deciding**: 5 referenda concurrently
- **Decision Deposit**: 500 QUAN
- **Prepare Period**: 12 hours
- **Decision Period**: 7 days
- **Confirm Period**: 12 hours
- **Minimum Enactment Period**: 12 hours

## Approval and Support Thresholds

### Minimum Approval
- Linear decreasing curve from 70% to 55%
- Requires 70% approval at the beginning of the voting period
- Requires at least 55% approval at the end

### Minimum Support
- Linear decreasing curve from 25% to 5%
- Requires at least 25% of the total possible votes at the beginning
- Minimum 5% of the total possible votes required by the end of the voting period

## Permissions

- Any account can submit and vote on proposals in this track
- Used for protocol changes that require privileged execution but lower security thresholds
- Ideal for parameter adjustments, non-critical upgrades, and mid-sized treasury disbursements
