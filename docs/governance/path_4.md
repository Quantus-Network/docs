# Path 4: Treasury Big Spender

## Parameters

- **Name**: big_spender
- **Maximum Deciding**: 1 referenda concurrently
- **Decision Deposit**: 1,000 QUAN
- **Prepare Period**: 48 hours
- **Decision Period**: 14 days
- **Confirm Period**: 48 hours
- **Minimum Enactment Period**: 48 hours

## Approval and Support Thresholds

### Minimum Approval
- Linear decreasing curve from 80% to 70%
- Requires 80% approval at the beginning of the voting period
- Requires at least 70% approval at the end

### Minimum Support
- Linear decreasing curve from 30% to 20%
- Requires at least 30% of the total possible votes at the beginning
- Minimum 20% of the total possible votes required by the end of the voting period

## Spending Limits

- Maximum amount per proposal: 20,000 QUAN
- Maximum total per period: 50,000 QUAN
- Minimum proposal amount: 5,000 QUAN

## Permissions

- Any account can submit and vote on proposals in this track
- Used for large treasury disbursements
- Ideal for significant community initiatives and partnerships
