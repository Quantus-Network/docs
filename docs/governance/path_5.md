# Path 5: Treasury Treasurer

## Parameters

- **Name**: treasurer
- **Maximum Deciding**: 1 referenda concurrently
- **Decision Deposit**: 2,000 QUAN
- **Prepare Period**: 72 hours
- **Decision Period**: 21 days
- **Confirm Period**: 72 hours
- **Minimum Enactment Period**: 72 hours

## Approval and Support Thresholds

### Minimum Approval
- Linear decreasing curve from 85% to 75%
- Requires 85% approval at the beginning of the voting period
- Requires at least 75% approval at the end

### Minimum Support
- Linear decreasing curve from 35% to 25%
- Requires at least 35% of the total possible votes at the beginning
- Minimum 25% of the total possible votes required by the end of the voting period

## Spending Limits

- Maximum amount per proposal: 50,000 QUAN
- Maximum total per period: 100,000 QUAN
- Minimum proposal amount: 20,000 QUAN

## Permissions

- Restricted to designated treasury treasurer accounts
- Used for significant treasury operations
- Ideal for major network initiatives and strategic partnerships
